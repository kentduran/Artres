Spotlight: Kent Duran

Kent Duran

Lead Products Designer & Creative Director

"I love creating tools that not only feel good to use but also inspire artists to explore new ideas and make art that truly resonates."

How Our Tools Come to Life

Kent Duran is the amazing force behind the fresh and smart art supplies at Artres. With over six years of experience in designing art materials, making fine art, and teaching, Kent brings a special blend of hands-on know-how and an artist's heart to everything we create. Kent holds a Master's degree in Industrial Design, focusing on how art materials are made.

Kent's main idea when designing is simple: tools should be easy to use, last a long time, and be kind to our planet. This means carefully looking into materials and how things are made, working closely with manufacturers. The goal is always to make sure every Artres product works wonderfully and is made with care for the environment.

Kent's Touch on Artres

  • Led the creation of our popular "Resonance Series" paint sets.
  • Designed easy-to-use digital art tablets.
  • Helped bring recycled and earth-friendly materials into our product packaging.
  • Chose the first set of unique artworks for the Artres Gallery.

Connect with Kent

Kent sometimes hosts online chats and writes for the Artres blog. Keep an eye out for chances to learn directly from Kent!
