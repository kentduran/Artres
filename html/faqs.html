Your Questions, Our Simple Answers

Artres offers a wonderful selection of quality art supplies for every artist. You'll find everything from bright acrylics, watercolors, and oil paints, to sketching pencils, blank canvases, and creative tools for digital art. We also have items for pottery, calligraphy, and many other art forms.

Yes, Artres sends art supplies to most countries around the world. The cost and time for delivery will depend on where you live. You can see all the details when you check out, or visit our Shipping Information page for more.

We want you to be happy with your Artres purchases! You can return items that haven't been used or damaged within 30 days of buying them for a full refund or exchange. Please note that custom-made items cannot be returned unless they have a defect. For easy steps on how to return something, please see our Returns & Exchanges page.

Right now, we don't have in-person classes, but we regularly share free tutorials and guides on our blog to help you learn. We're also planning to launch online workshops and courses very soon! Make sure you sign up for our newsletter to get all the latest updates.

Once your art supplies are on their way, we'll send you an email. This email will include a tracking number and a link to the shipping company's website, so you can easily follow your package's journey.

At Artres, we care deeply about our planet. We're always working to offer more products that are kind to the earth and made in responsible ways. We also choose to work with suppliers who share our values for a healthier environment. Look for special notes on product pages to see which items are eco-friendly.
