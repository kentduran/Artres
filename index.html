Who We Are

Artres is more than just an art supply store; it's a community built on the belief that everyone holds a unique spark of creativity. We started this journey to make sure every artist, whether just beginning or already skilled, has access to the best tools and a supportive space. Our team is made up of artists, designers, and educators who genuinely love the act of creation. We're here to share that passion with you, offering carefully chosen art supplies, practical tips, and a friendly corner of the internet to share your work.

Each item in our collection, from vibrant paints to smooth sketchbooks, is picked with care. Our main goal is simple: to help your artistic ideas blossom and to inspire you to create art that truly resonates. We're not just selling products; we're helping you connect with the joy and impact of making something truly your own.

Our Mission

At Artres, our main goal is to light up and keep alive the creative spirit in everyone. We aim to be your favorite place for excellent art supplies, helpful learning materials, and a friendly community that cheers you on as you explore and grow. We are here to make art simple, fun, and truly fulfilling for everyone, no matter if you're just starting or have been creating for years.

We work to remove anything that might get in the way of your creativity. We provide new and helpful tools and create a space where artists can truly "create art that resonates" – art that touches hearts, makes people think, and connects deeply with others.
